About the Project

First of all, I'd like to thank all those that participated in this project by taking the time to send in photos. My goal with this project was to showcase different people around the world by using two very simple yet different questions. The prompts were "What is something that gives you joy?" and "What is something that overwhelms you?". These emotions are felt universally and go cross cultures: everyone feels happiness and they get overwhelmed. I picked these two questions so I would have two emotions on opposite sides of the spectrum. As the responses came in, I found that although there were many similarities, everyone had their own unique take. For joy many people stuck to what I expected, their families. However, I was intrigued by some of the images I got back. It shows that everyone has their own definition of joy and what it means to them. The same thing goes for overwhelmed except I didn't really have an expectation for that. Some were overwhelmed by spiders but others said they were overwhelmed by expectations they set for themselves. All in all, this collection of photos and information goes to show how a simple question can have so many different interpretations and responses. Enjoy looking through the project :)
